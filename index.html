The Symposium

Powerful Talk followed by Breakout Sessions

May 19th 2024

09:00 am

The purpose of the event is to inspire youth to further their interest in genetics, highlight recent advances in genetics, and show the breadth and expansive nature of the field. The intended audience is local high school students and community members with an aim of 100 participants.

The event will consist of a brief overview of genetics, a keynote speaker, an industry presentation, breakout rooms discussing interdisciplinary fields within genetics, and a panel discussion with all of the breakout speakers.
