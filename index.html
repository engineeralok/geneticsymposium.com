The Symposium

Keynote Speeches followed by Breakout Sessions

May 19th 2024

9:00 am

The purpose of the event is to inspire youth to further their interest in genetics, highlight recent advances in genetics, and show the breadth and expansive nature of the field. The intended audience is local high school students and interested community members.

The event will consist of a brief overview of genetics, two keynote speakers, an industry presentation, breakout rooms discussing interdisciplinary fields within genetics, and a panel discussion with all of the breakout speakers.

About Genetics club

What is Genetics club?

Genetics club is a Los Altos High School approved club focusing on creating awareness, education, through on campus events and off campus events open to the community. We are a group of students that are excited by the continued advancements in the field and feel it will be one of our generations' greatest advancements. Some of us know little about the field, some of us have extensive knowledge, but all of us are curious and want to learn more. Thank you for considering supporting our event by your attendence, questions, and potentially a sponsorship for this and future events.
